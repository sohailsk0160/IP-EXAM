Typography • Contrast • Layout

Design with readable type and rich backgrounds

Use the right font-family and weights for hierarchy, add subtle text-shadow for legibility over images, and pair solid backgrounds with gradients or images for depth.

Font Family & Weights

Try switching font-family between a serif and a sans-serif and use font-weight to create hierarchy (300 light — 800 extra bold).

Text Transform & Spacing

Properties: text-transform, letter-spacing, word-spacing. Use letter-spacing for uppercase headings and word-spacing for improving paragraph rhythm.

Text Decoration & Shadow

Use text-decoration for links, and text-shadow to lift text off bright images (apply sparingly).

Example note that may truncate on small widths: A very long line of text that demonstrates text-overflow behaviour when a container gets narrow.

Background Techniques

Solid colors, gradients, images, and layered backgrounds: combine them with background-size, background-position, and background-attachment.

Try: background: linear-gradient(90deg,#fefefe88,#ffffff00), url(...)
